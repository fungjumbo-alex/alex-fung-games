How to Play

Objective: Catch falling oil drops with workers before they hit the ground!

Controls:

• Arrow Keys ← → or Buttons to move the top worker

• Swipe left/right on canvas for mobile

Scoring:

• +10 points for successfully passing oil to bottom

• 3 misses = Game Over
