How to Play

Objective: Catch falling villagers with your net and avoid fireballs!

Controls:

• Arrow Keys or WASD to move up/down floors

• D-pad buttons for touch control

• Space to pause

Scoring:

• +10 points per villager saved

• 3 misses = Game Over
