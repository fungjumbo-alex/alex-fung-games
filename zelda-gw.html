How to Play

Objective: Navigate dungeons, defeat enemies, find the exit!

Controls:

• Arrow Keys or D-pad to move

• Space or ⚔️ button to attack

Scoring:

• +10 points per rupee

• +25 points per enemy defeated

• Collect keys to open doors!

• 3 hearts = Game Over
