How to Play

Objective: Catch falling food with your pan and toss it back to the shelf!

Controls:

• Arrow Keys ← → or Buttons to move

• Swipe left/right for mobile

Scoring:

• +10 points for catching food

• +5 points for successful toss back

• 3 misses = Game Over
